What is AI as a service? AI as a service (AIaaS) is a service offered by third-party vendors that allows businesses to incorporate AI-powered tools and capabilities into their systems. AIaaS is a low-risk and cost-effective model because businesses can deploy AI without investing in resources to build and implement it from scratch.
