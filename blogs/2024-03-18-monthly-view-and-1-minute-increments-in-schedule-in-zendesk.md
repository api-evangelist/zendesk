<p>It&#8217;s simple: when you need them, you really need them. That&#8217;s why we are excited about these new options that will make your life scheduling more adaptable.</p>
<p>You can now change your scheduled working time down to the minute and see schedules by the month. Now that&#8217;s what we&#8217;re talking about.</p>
<h3>What&#8217;s in it for you</h3>
<p><strong>1-minute increments for your schedules:</strong></p>
<p>From time to time, we found that teams needed more options when scheduling. Though it&#8217;s common to see shifts start on the hour or half hour, what about the teams that need their agents to come in 10 minutes early for a team huddle at the beginning of the shift? Or the teams that are required to meet a particular amount of minutes and hours a week for particular tasks?</p>
<p>Well, now Tymeshift has you covered. Pick any time, down to the minute, to schedule your agents. Do you need someone to jump on the chat channel at 10:02 am next Tuesday &#8211; done. It may seem overly specific now, but when you need it, you really need it.</p>

<p><strong>Monthly view:</strong></p>
<p>This one may seem more obvious because, let&#8217;s be real, what scheduling manager doesn&#8217;t love a monthly view?</p>
<p>You can now click to see your team&#8217;s schedule by month, not just week or day. Life-changing? Maybe. Wildly convenient? Definitely.</p>
